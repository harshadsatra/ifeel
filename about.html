iFEEL Institute of Management studies

iFEEL aims to help the student develop their existing, passion, knowledge and skills, by providing them with superlative faculty, who are instrumental in cultivating their talents and directing their passion, to the path of becoming the global leaders. The objective of this institute is to produce entrepreneurs who innovate, challenge the existing conventions by creating new paradigms in the field of management. Its mission is to create a talent pool of competent and excellent professionals, who areequipped to become the world class leaders in near future. This institute offers students a wide range of courses that will broaden their scope of knowledge and vocational training.

Courses like, the Post Graduate Diploma in Management - PGDM has the flexibility to incorporate the new trends in business while still providing basic management education. iFEEL also offers, 2 years Full-Time AICTE Approved PGDM Program which has a special focus on Entrepreneurship and Leadership excellence that takes into account industry suggestions and feedback. It has adopted 'Industry-Integrated Curriculum' combined with Leadership and Entrepreneurship development.

This institute is mentored by Welingkar Institute of Management (WeSchool), which is one of the Best Management Institute in India. This association has helped us in not just developing quality content, but also providing the corporate exposure to the students. In today's scenario, the corporate world is seeking professionals who are much more than mere MBA's. They are looking out for the 'Torch-bearers of Tomorrow' - those who are not just academically sound, but also possess qualities of innovation and leadership; those who have the passion to excel in all walks of life and have the determination to emerge as winners; those who don't just have great ideas but can also implement them.

Experience and Vision

Post Graduate Management education is a key contributor for acquiring desired business knowledge, entrepreneurship and leadership qualities. This competitive environment demands competent, innovative and skilled leaders. iFEEL’s business model is designed keeping in mind the challenges and uncertainties of the contemporary corporate sector, that will shape the minds of the budding managers, to look beyond the regular paradigms of business for solutions. It has structured its courses in programs after studying the dynamic, trends, practices of International business for creating insightful entrepreneurs who could lead the world. The vision of the institute is to create leading entrepreneurs, who would direct the course of management, by imparting them quality education and training. iFEEL not only aims to foster entrepreneurial and leadership qualities among the students but they also believe in creating an intellectual pool with the talents that comes to the institute. Therefore the group director rightly addressed to all the students by stating “Choosing the right management program is one of the most important decisions that you will make in your life. You have to be sure that you opt for the best and the latest.” Hence this institute is the apt choice for any student who believes in revamping the face of management, by becoming an insightful, excellent and innovative entrepreneur.

Placements

Getting placed in a leading company is every student’s desire and iFEEL is fulfilling them since its inception. It not only train the students for the corporate and management world but also ensures their placements in one of the leading companies where they can utilize and make most of their acquired quality knowledge, skill, talent and training in these blue chip companies. From the beginning, this institute has been placing the students in top-notch companies for jobs and summer internships and therefore it guarantees 100% placements to the students who became a part of it.
