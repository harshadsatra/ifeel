Eligibility Criteria

10th/ 12th and Graduation from a UGC recognized university

Entrance Exam – (optional) CAT’16/ XAT’17/ MHCET

Selection Process

  • GRADUATION AGGREGATE 50%
  • APTITUDE TEST
  • WRITTEN ABILITY TEST
  • PERSONAL INTERVIEW
  • GROUP DISCUSSION

After Results

1. Incase Selected: Students have to pay retention fees amount Rs. 60k (within 7 working days after offer letter softcopy received)

2. Incase On Hold: Students have to wait for 15 days and then again reply or connect to us for further information. The Eligibility mentioned below is for the 2 year Full-Time, AICTE approved PGDM Program

Aptitude / Interview

iFEEL will conduct Apptitute Test followed by 2 rounds of Personal Interview at selected cities across India. Students can select the nearest centre to go through these evaluation procedures for admission to iFEEL. The institute can at any time, change or cancel the venue / timings. It is mandatory for students to have appeared in any one of the following entrance exams before appearing for the PI:- CAT 2015, XAT 2016, ATMA15-16, MAT 15-16, C-MAT 2015-16 and MH CET 2016.

Personal Interview (PI) on the other hand, conducted for the admission process is entirely different from the GD, therefore a set pattern is definitely not possible. However, making a list of common questions that were asked in the previous years can really help in preparing for the personal interview. Confidence is very vital to pass the interview and if you have adequate knowledge, then you have maximum probability to make a strong positive impact on the interviewer.
