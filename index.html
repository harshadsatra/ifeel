Welcome to iFEEL

Institute for Future Education,
Entrepreneurship and Leadership.

The future of Management Education and Entrepreneurship is in IFEEL, an institute located at Lonavala, offering students with serene and amazing campus. It is a management institute that takes students much beyond the scope of the conventional PGDM Programs.

iFEEL offers students an excellent environment, which is conducive to create world-class leaders, who are competent enough to change the course of management by taking it to the higher dimension. iFEEL is committed to, producing Leaders and Entrepreneurs through contemporary and comprehensive business management courses and that have the passion for innovation and changing the face of the world.

VISION

iFEEL’s Vision is to create an innovative, quality-driven institution that produces leading entrepreneurs

  • To be a leading management institution
  • To be the nurturer of leadership and entrepreneurship excellence
  • To create competent talent that can direct the course of management

MISSION

  • By providing world class faculty to disseminate top-notch management education that will create professionals who have global standing
  • To undertake research and consultancy for the betterment of the industry and society
  • To continuously evolve and innovate process to cultivate the existing talent into leading entrepreneurs.
  • By building an intellectual pool of students by creating a quality-driven and innovative environment

CLASSROOMS

Space to broaden the scope of Knowledge

iFEEL has facilitated students with modern classrooms that are conducive to create leading professionals, with innovative thoughts and processes. The classrooms are spacious and well-equipped with technology that will help the faculties to implement new teaching methodologies, which will nurtureand cultivate the talent, passion, and knowledge to produce outstanding entrepreneurs. Classrooms at iFEEL are spacious and luminous, with comfortable seats and well-connected to wi-fi, so that the students are updated with innovations and new trends that occur in the outer world. It is designed to break the notion of the conventional classrooms, as the blackboards are replaced by screens, transforming them into learning and research field.

RAIN LAB / INNO VATION LAB

Reflect, act, innovate and nurture.

Rain lab is the place where students churn their creative juices to innovate and create new things. It is a creative area designed for students to innovate and formulate strategies and ideas that will help them to develop and discover their creative side. It is to motivate students to collectively come up with a new plethora of idea that can be feasibly implemented for different products. Rain lab was inaugurated in February 2012 by Dr. Sten Ekman.

COMPUTER LAB

Technology helps change conventions

Computer lab at iFEEL is equipped with the state- of-the- art technology. The computer lab is the place where the growing entrepreneurs are innovating and developing strategies to challenge the current conventions. The computer lab has an amazing connectivity with unlimited wifi, helping the student explore and widen the scope of learning which helps them to gain a different perspective and create something new.

LANGUAGE TUTORIAL

Communication is a key to build business

iFEEL focuses on the overall development of the student. Communication is the base of any business. In iFEEL, students are trained to develop and enhance their communication skills. There is an offering of courses that will make students proficient and fluent in foreign languages like German and Japanese.

LIBRARY

Intellect feeds on Knowledge

Library is a necessity of any students to find sources for completion of their research. iFEEL offers students an upgraded library with books, that will give them exposure and easy access to update their knowledge, by keeping them abreast about the field of management. This is going to make the future entrepreneurs nimble by broadening their scope of learning. This upgraded library is a resource that will aid iFEEL in creating an intellectual pool with talented future professionals.

HOSTELS

Homely spaces within the campus

iFEEL’s campus offers outstation students with well-furnished hostels so that they don’t have to go hunting for accommodation outside. It is equipped with best in class amenities to comfort and suit their needs. Every room in the hostel is designed with keeping in mind the needs of the students making it spacious and hygienic to add the homely touch to it. There are separate hostels for girls and boys within the campus.

MAIN OFFICE

Resolve your official queries here!

The staff of iFEEL is present in the main office and its job is to guide students by solving queries and resolving their issues. Detailed information about the institute and courses offered can be gained from this place.

SPORTS ACTIVITIES

Wide spaces to indulge in sports

iFEEL emphasizes the importance of healthy body, along with healthy mind, for an overall character development. Therefore, it has facilitated students with a campus that has a ground and activity areas allocated for them to indulge in various sports. There is a badminton court, as well as a Throw ball court, and enough space for students to indulge in other athletic activities. iFEEL encourages students to indulge in various sports activities, like Carom, and Table Tennis, which are played by the students in the activity area. Various unconventional sports event are organized within the campus, that are quite unique from the regular sports competition, to encourage participation, awareness regarding the importance of health; and to inculcate love for sports among student. Students participate in these events with a lot of zeal and enthusiasm. These competitions are divided into two categories- On Ground and Off Ground. On Ground consist of all the competitions that can be played on the ground like chess while off ground consists of competitions that are conducted inside the activity area like carom and table tennis tournaments.

CAMPUS VENTURES

Great ideas, great coffee

Coffee and refreshments are something that every curious brain craves for. iFEEL is the place where the students are nurtured to be innovative and curious and they require a caffeine kick for energizing their brains. Therefore one of the student came up with an amazing idea of offering quick coffee breaks by installing the coffee machine within the campus. This has now developed into a venture and students are enjoying coffee and paying for it.

GCL

Competitions that are real-life work experience

Global Citizen Leaders (GCL) was conducted within the campus iFEEL, on 28th and 29th March. The program focused on acquainting the students with the varied real-time business scenarios, through live projects and presentations. 12 teams from the PGDM batch of 2015-2017 had participated in the event and undertaken projects from various companies. Some of the companies included Ador Group, TATA Rallis, Da Milano, Lishi Laser, etc.

Team Zealous won the first prize for their project named Ador-Food in all the categories The jury of the event included Mr.Christer Nygren, Ms. Anette Strömberg and Ms.Mona Tjärnberg, who are all associated with Mälardalen University, Sweden. The luminaries provided the students with useful insights regarding dealing with real-time business scenarios and overcoming the hurdles in doing the same.

RESEARCH

Research is the key to knowledge

Every Entrepreneur has to have the knowledge of his business. This knowledge is gained only from learning and doing thorough research on the subject. These spaces are research rooms which are spacious, luminous, and well furnished with sitting arrangements for students to do their study and basic research about the field of management to get a better understanding of the dynamic of the arena of management.

Amphitheatre is method to decipher knowledge to students which is beyond teachings of syllabus and classrooms.
